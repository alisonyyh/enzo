Support

Need help with Enzo? We're here for you. Whether you're having trouble with your puppy's routine, your account, or anything else in the app, reach out and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

Contact Us

For any questions, issues, or feedback, email us at hello@getenzo.app. We typically respond within 24 hours.

What We Can Help With

  • Account issues (signing in, updating your profile)
  • Routine setup and customization
  • Push notification problems
  • Bug reports and app feedback
  • Data deletion requests

Reporting a Bug

Please include your app version and device model when reporting a bug. You can find your app version in Settings.
