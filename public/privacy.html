Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

Enzo ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mobile application that helps you generate and track daily routines for your puppy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account. We use this solely to create and manage your Enzo account.

Puppy and routine data. You provide information about your puppy (such as name, breed, age, and weight) and your routine preferences. We store your task schedules, task completion history, and weight logs so the app can function as intended.

Usage analytics. We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage data such as page views, button clicks, and feature usage. This helps us understand how the app is used and where to make improvements. This data is not sold or shared with advertisers.

Push notification tokens. If you enable push notifications, we collect a device token through Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver notifications to your device. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app
  • Generate and manage your puppy's routine
  • Send push notifications you've opted into
  • Understand usage patterns to improve the app experience
  • Respond to support requests

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication
  • Supabase — for data storage
  • Firebase (Google) — for Firestore database and push notifications
  • PostHog — for usage analytics

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers operated by Supabase and Firebase. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing hello@getenzo.app. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will permanently remove your account and data within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Enzo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@getenzo.app and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Choices

You can disable push notifications through your device settings at any time. You can request a copy of your data or request account deletion by emailing hello@getenzo.app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by other means. The "Effective date" at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at hello@getenzo.app.
